Nigeria

Nigeria, located in West Africa, is a vibrant and diverse country with a rich tapestry of cultures, languages, and traditions. Boasting a population of over 200 million people, Nigeria is the most populous country in Africa, offering a dynamic blend of ethnicities, religions, and landscapes. From bustling urban centers like Lagos, the commercial capital, to the scenic beauty of places like the Jos Plateau and the Niger Delta, Nigeria showcases a diverse range of environments. Renowned for its music, literature, and cuisine, Nigeria's cultural influence extends far beyond its borders.
